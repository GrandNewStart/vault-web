Vault – Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 12, 2025 (Asia/Seoul)

Vault is a personal data “vault” app focused on privacy and security. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

On by default: Private-by-design

Summary

  • Today, Vault collects no personal data from you or your device. No analytics, no ads, no tracking pixels, no cookies.
  • Your content is processed and stored on-device unless you explicitly export or share it.
  • In the future (see Future data collection), Vault may collect minimal identifiers that you provide (e.g., a push notification token or a public key) to enable optional features like push notifications and peer‑to‑peer (P2P) message delivery.

What we collect today

Nothing. The app does not send or collect any personal information, usage analytics, or device identifiers from your device to our servers.

On‑device storage

Your data (e.g., files, keys, settings) is stored locally on your device or in your platform’s secure storage (e.g., Keychain / Keystore) and remains under your control.

No network exfiltration

The app does not transmit data to us unless you explicitly connect to a feature that needs it in the future (see below). There is no third‑party advertising or tracking SDK.

Future data collection (opt‑in features)

To support optional features, we may ask you to provide limited data. We will update this policy and present in‑app notices before these features go live.

Data we may request

  • Push Notification Token – a device‑specific token from Apple/Google used solely to deliver notifications you opt into.
  • Public Keys – cryptographic public keys you choose to upload or register so other users/services can send you encrypted messages or establish secure P2P channels.

How we use this data

  • Push delivery – associate your opted‑in device with a topic or account to send relevant push notifications.
  • P2P routing & discovery – enable other parties to look up your public key (never your private key) to exchange encrypted messages.

What we still won’t do

  • Sell your data.
  • Use it for advertising or profiling.
  • Collect private keys or unencrypted content.

Legal bases & purposes

We will process future optional data under one or more of the following legal bases (depending on your region):

  • Consent (e.g., you opt in to push notifications or register a public key).
  • Contract (e.g., providing the service features you request).
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., protecting our services from abuse, provided your rights are not overridden).

Retention

  • On‑device data stays until you delete the app data or uninstall the app.
  • Push tokens & public keys (future) are kept only as long as needed to provide the feature or until you delete or revoke them.

Security

  • We design Vault to minimize data leaving your device.
  • We use platform security features (e.g., Keychain/Keystore, secure enclave when available).
  • For any future server‑side components, we will use encryption in transit (TLS) and apply industry‑standard security practices.

Sharing & transfers

  • No sale of data.
  • No advertising use.
  • We may use essential service providers (e.g., push delivery infrastructure) strictly to operate requested features, under appropriate safeguards and data‑processing agreements.

Your choices

  • Stay offline – continue using Vault without enabling optional network features.
  • Opt in/out – enable or disable push notifications at any time in system settings and in‑app controls (when available).
  • Manage keys – add or remove public keys; revoke lookups or discovery (when applicable).
  • Export/Erase – export your on‑device data or delete it by clearing app data/uninstalling the app.

Children’s privacy

Vault is not directed to children under the age where parental consent is required in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as we evolve Vault or add optional features. We will post updates here and, where appropriate, in the app. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent changes.

Contact

If you have questions or requests (access, deletion, portability, objection), contact us at hbjw123@gmail.com. We will respond as required by applicable law.
